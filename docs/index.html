03pillars

What it stands on.

Frontline contact IS the substrate.
Agents mapped to jobs.
Humans hold the checkpoints.
Context is the load-bearing layer.
Narrative is the strategy.
Status quo is the competitor.
Measurement opens the work, not closes it.
Distribution is the moat.

Eight pillars. Each cited by 4 to 8 operators

Nothing is invention.
