Trial validated, team aligned, CFO approved budget, just needs friction-free contract + onboarding date.

## Daily reality

## Goals (top 3, ranked)
1. Sign by Friday.
2. Onboard 8 reps next Monday.
3. Hit Q3 quota with the new tool live.

## Frictions (top 5)
- Order form doesn't show the discount she negotiated.
- Auto-renewal clause is 60 days, she wants 30.
- SOC 2 report requires a separate request.
- Onboarding date isn't bookable until contract signs.
- AE is going on PTO Thursday.

- nice-to-have: same-day onboarding kickoff, rep training hours included, AI summary on day 1.
- deal-breaker: contract surprises.
