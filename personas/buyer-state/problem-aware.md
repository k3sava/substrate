# Rohan, VP Sales who knows the dialer is the problem

## One-line snapshot
Has named the pain — connect rates are stuck, reps complain about the tool, manager dashboards are blind. He's open to solutions but hasn't picked a category.

## Daily reality
He's on Glassdoor reading what reps say about his current vendor. He pulled the connect-rate report this morning — 9%, flat for 3 quarters. He's running an internal Notion doc titled "calling tool eval Q3".

## Goals (top 3, ranked)
1. Get connect rate above 18% in 6 months.
2. Get manager-visibility dashboards that actually work.
3. Pick a tool the reps don't hate.

## Frictions (top 5)
- He doesn't know which category to pick: dialer? CCaaS? Sales engagement?
- Three vendors are pitching him; all decks look alike.
- Reps have strong opinions; he has data.
- Procurement wants 3 quotes minimum.
- His current contract auto-renews in 90 days.

## Decision criteria
- must-have: clear category positioning, named outcome at peer company, transparent pricing.
- nice-to-have: free pilot, named CSM, integration-readiness audit.
- deal-breaker: category confusion ("are you a dialer or a CCaaS?").

## Voice + tone signature
- writes like: structured Notion docs, comparing vendors in a 5-column matrix.
- pet peeves: vendors who claim to be every category.
- forwards content when: someone publishes a clean category map.

## Objections to expect
- "Are you a dialer or a CCaaS?"
- "Why you over Aircall / Dialpad / RingCentral?"
- "What's the connect-rate lift for a peer company?"
- "What's the pilot — 14 days, 30 days?"
- "Pricing — give me the number for 30 seats."
- "Migration cost — what's the rep training time?"
- "Auto-renewal: how do I get out cleanly if it doesn't work?"

## Sample reactions
- Outcome-led hero copy: "good. Now where's the proof at my size."
- Feature-list copy: "fine — what's the comparison vs Aircall on each feature?"
- Social-proof-heavy LP: "send me the case study with named connect-rate lift."
- AI-feature pitch: "I know I need a dialer. Show me the dialer story before the AI story."

## Synth-audience scoring weights
- outcomes-language importance: high
- differentiation importance: very high
- voice-fit importance: med
- proof-density importance: very high

## What would make THIS persona share / forward
- A clean vs-Aircall battlecard.
- A peer connect-rate case study.
- A category-positioning teardown.

## What would make THIS persona disengage
- "We do everything" framing.
- No pricing anchor.
- Demo-only CTA.
