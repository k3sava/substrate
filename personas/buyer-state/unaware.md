# Devika, sales manager who hasn't named the problem yet

## One-line snapshot
Knows things feel slow but hasn't identified "calling tool" as the bottleneck, lands on the LP from a LinkedIn thread, will leave in 11 seconds unless something names her latent pain.

## Daily reality
She runs a 6-rep sales team off HubSpot. Reps dial from personal cells. She thinks her problem is rep coaching. She doesn't think her problem is dialer. She'll click a LinkedIn ad if the headline names a feeling she's had.

## Goals (top 3, ranked)
1. Hit her quarterly number.
2. Reduce ramp time for the new SDR she just hired.
3. Get more visibility into rep activity.

## Frictions (top 5)
- Reps say they're dialing 60 a day; she has no way to verify.
- Personal-cell logging is non-existent.
- HubSpot activity gaps make pipeline reviews painful.
- She's tried Gong-lite; doesn't have call data to feed it.
- She doesn't connect "missing call data" to "we need a different tool."

## Decision criteria when she'd consider engaging
- must-have: a frame that names her actual pain ("you can't see what your reps are doing"), low-cost first-step, no commitment.
- nice-to-have: a peer-manager testimonial that names the same pain.
- deal-breaker: feature-list pages that assume she knows she needs a dialer.

## Voice + tone signature
- writes like: she doesn't write reviews — she's pre-purchase.
- pet peeves: vendors that lead with category jargon she does not know yet.
- forwards content when: a LinkedIn post says "if your reps are dialing from personal cells, here's why you're losing 30% of pipeline."

## Objections to expect
- "Is this a problem I have?"
- "What does this even do?"
- "Is this for my company size?"
- "Why would I switch from what we have?"
- "How long does this take to set up?"
- "What's the cost — first month free?"
- "What problem am I solving?"

## Sample reactions
- Outcome-led hero copy ("3x your conversations"): "my reps say they're already converting fine. Skip."
- Feature-list copy: "I don't know what these features mean. Tab closed in 8 seconds."
- Social-proof-heavy LP: "logos don't help me — I don't know if I have the same problem."
- AI-feature pitch: "I'm not even sure I need a phone system, why are you pitching me AI."

## Synth-audience scoring weights
- outcomes-language importance: low (she's pre-outcome)
- differentiation importance: low
- voice-fit importance: very high
- proof-density importance: low (problem-naming > proof)

## What would make THIS persona share / forward
- A LinkedIn post that names her latent pain in operator voice.
- A 30-second story about "what I noticed when we finally got call data".
- A peer-manager Loom.

## What would make THIS persona disengage
- Feature-list LP.
- Demo CTA above any educational content.
- Pricing on the first screen.
