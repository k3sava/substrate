# Linda, Ops Manager at a 32-tech multi-trade HVAC + plumbing shop

## One-line snapshot
Runs dispatch and the 6-CSR call center for an owner-operated home services business, evaluates phone systems on whether they catch missed calls and book the same day.

## Daily reality
She's at the desk at 6:30am routing the morning's first calls. ServiceTitan is on one screen, the call queue dashboard on the other. By 11am she's chasing techs who didn't update their job status. After-hours call routing is a constant pain — voicemail piles up at 6pm and nobody listens until 8am.

## Goals (top 3, ranked)
1. Book every inbound call into a same-day or next-day slot.
2. Cut after-hours missed calls by 80%.
3. Stop losing calls to abandoned voicemail.

## Frictions (top 5)
- After-hours calls go to voicemail. 30% never call back.
- IVR "press 1 for service, press 2 for billing" loses customers in 8 seconds.
- ServiceTitan call sync drops when the techs are on cellular.
- CSRs juggle inbound + outbound + texts — there's no unified queue.
- The owner wants AI to handle overflow but Linda doesn't trust it on her customers.

## Decision criteria when evaluating phone systems
- must-have: ServiceTitan native integration, after-hours auto-text-back with appointment link, one CSR queue across voice + SMS.
- nice-to-have: AI agent that books simple appointments, call recording, busy-hour overflow.
- deal-breaker: AI agent that books wrong type of service or wrong tech.

## Voice + tone signature
- writes like: practical, no-nonsense. "We tried that, it didn't work because..." Specific examples. G2 reviews are 60 words and end with star count justification.
- pet peeves: vendors who pitch "voice AI" without acknowledging that 70% of her calls are not standard appointments.
- forwards content when: a peer in Service Titan's Pantheon community posts a missed-call recovery workflow.

## Objections to expect
- "Does it work with ServiceTitan or do I have to wait for an integration?"
- "If the AI agent books a tune-up but the customer needs an emergency, what happens?"
- "How does the missed-call text-back look? Custom or canned?"
- "Can I set quiet hours by tech, by trade, by zone?"
- "What's the per-CSR cost? My owner won't sign more than $X."
- "If the AI agent fails, does the call go to voicemail or to a CSR?"
- "Show me an HVAC shop my size with a real number."

## Sample reactions
- Outcome-led hero copy: "fine, but in home services I need 'booked appointments,' not 'conversations.'"
- Feature-list copy: "where's missed-call recovery? if it's not the headline I question whether you've worked with home services."
- Social-proof-heavy LP: "B2B SaaS logos. not for me. send me Yellowstone Local."
- AI-feature pitch: "I want to see the AI agent fail gracefully. Show me the fallback handoff video."
