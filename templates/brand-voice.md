# Brand voice

How we write. Cadence rules, kill-list words, pet phrases.

## Cadence

Sentence-length variance per Gary Provost: mix short, medium, and longer sentences.

Target band: 14-20 average words per sentence, with deliberate variance inside.

## Banned in body copy

- Em dashes — use commas, periods, colons, parens instead.
- Throat-clearing openers ("In today's fast-paced world", "It's no secret that", "We all know that", "Have you ever wondered").

## Outcome-led

Open with what the reader gets. Features come after. "Ship invoices in 90 seconds" before "Our invoice generator."

## Active voice, default

"The user clicked the button" not "The button was clicked by the user." Default active.

## Specific over generic

"Outbound SDR teams running 5-50 reps" beats "Sales teams". When the audience is broad, put the specific noun in the next sentence.
