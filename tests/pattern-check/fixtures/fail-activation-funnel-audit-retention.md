# Q2 onboarding update

The onboarding flow is finally finished. Activation is up this quarter, retention is solid, and the team is feeling good about it.

## Onboarding completion

We tightened the onboarding flow from 7 steps to 5 and the completion rate went up. Activation rate is now 41% week over week. Most users finish onboarding within a day. The team thinks this is great.

## Retention

Retention this quarter is 70%, up from 65% last quarter. We are not sure what drove the lift but it might be the onboarding work. The activation team is going to think about what comes next.

## What is next

We will probably look at expansion next, since retention is healthy. The activation rate dropped slightly the last week of the month but is otherwise stable. We had an aha moment on a brainstorming call about the next signup form.

## Recommendation

Ship the redesigned onboarding to all users. Expand the activation team headcount. The numbers look good.
