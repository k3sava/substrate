# Q3 ads recap

The paid budget is up 12% this quarter and the team is feeling good about the new creative concepts. Some campaigns are doing well and some need attention. Here is the recap.

## Spend summary

We spent $410K on Meta and $190K on Google. CAC across the program looks reasonable. The team is going to refresh creatives sometime in the next month and probably look at the channel mix at the next quarterly review.

## What is working

The new "summer launch" creative on Meta drove some good-looking conversion numbers. Google search is steady. We had an aha moment on a brainstorming call: the buyers really seem to like the new tagline. We will keep using it.

## What needs attention

Several campaigns are tired. We should think about whether to do A/B tests on the next batch of subject lines and ad copy. We should also separate the budgets between teams to make planning cleaner.

## Recommendation

Approve another 15% increase on Meta. The platform-reported ROAS looks fine. If anything goes wrong we can pull spend back and the team will figure out the next steps.
